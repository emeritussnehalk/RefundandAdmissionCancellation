  9. Escalation: I acknowledge that if my refund request exceeds 15 business days without completion, I may raise an escalation through the provided channel. Escalation does not guarantee expedited processing but ensures additional review.
  10. Finality & Acceptance: I acknowledge that submission of this request constitutes a formal request for admission cancellation and/or refund. The institution’s decision, taken in accordance with the applicable refund policy and these terms, shall be final and binding.

Refund/ Cancellation Withdrawal Policy & Terms

By choosing to withdraw a Refund and/or Admission Cancellation request, I acknowledge and agree to the following terms and conditions:
  1. Eligibility to Withdraw
    Only the student who submitted the refund request is permitted to withdraw it. Withdrawal is permitted only until the refund request has been sent for processing. Once the request reaches a non-withdrawable stage, the withdrawal option will no longer be available.
  2. Effect of Withdrawal
    Upon withdrawal, the refund request will be permanently closed. No refund will be processed after withdrawal. All request details, including refund calculations and policy references, will be archived for audit and compliance purposes.
  3. Irrevocability & Reapplication
    Withdrawal of a refund request is final and irreversible. A withdrawn request cannot be reopened or reinstated. If I wish to pursue a refund after withdrawal, I understand that I must submit a fresh refund request as a new application.
  4. Policy Applicability on Reapplication
    I acknowledge that any fresh refund request submitted after withdrawal will be governed by the refund policy applicable on the new Date Applied. I understand that the refund amount, eligibility, and charges for a reapplication may differ from my original request based on the prevailing policy at the time of reapplication.
  5. Policy & Date Locking
    For every refund request, the applicable refund policy is determined and locked based on the Date Applied. Withdrawal does not alter or invalidate the historical policy and refund calculation stored for audit.
  6. Lending Partner & Split Payment Cases
    If my payment includes a lending partner (loan), I acknowledge that any coordination already initiated with the lending partner may be discontinued upon withdrawal, where feasible. Any obligations, settlements, or communications with the lending partner remain my sole responsibility. In cases of split payments (Self+Loan), withdrawal applies to the entire refund request, and no partial refunds will be processed.
  7. Communications & Record
    Upon successful withdrawal, I will receive a confirmation communication containing Ticket ID and date/time of withdrawal. All withdrawal actions are time-stamped, user-validated, and logged for compliance and audit.
  8. Limitation of Liability
    After withdrawal, the institution and platform shall have no obligation or liability to process or pursue any refund related to the withdrawn request. Any subsequent refund request will be treated as a new, independent request.
